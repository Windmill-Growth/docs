## What you can manage

In **Settings** \> **Billing**, you can manage:

- your current subscription
- credit usage history
- payment methods
- billing history
- plan changes
- current-month Pay-as-you-go spend

Your workspace sidebar also shows credit usage so you can track consumption without leaving your day-to-day workflow.

Everyone in the workspace can follow usage from the product, but owners and admins manage billing changes.

## Use the main billing areas

Billing & Plans includes the public areas most teams need:

- **Overview** for your current plan and billing period
- **Credit Usage** for the billing-window credit ledger
- **Payment Methods** for saved cards
- **Billing History** for invoices
- **Manage Subscription** for upgrades, downgrades, and interval changes

## How periods and resets work

Included credits reset monthly.

When credits are low or unavailable, some product actions can pause until:

- your next monthly reset
- you update billing settings
- you change plan or credit behavior

Use the billing summary in your workspace as the source of truth for the next reset date and remaining usage.

## Review credit usage history

Open **Credit Usage** when you want a ledger of credit charges in the current billing window.

Use it to:

- review qualification, post run, and enrichment charges
- search by person, post, or lead details
- filter by activity type, credit type, and date range
- sort and page through billing-window activity

## Common billing tasks

Use the billing area when you need to:

- review current plan details
- review credit usage history
- update a card
- view invoices and payment history
- change subscription level
- open the billing portal for additional billing tasks

## Handle payment issues

If a renewal or upgrade payment fails, Billing & Plans shows recovery guidance in the billing area.

Use it to:

- retry checkout verification if Stripe sync is still catching up
- update your payment method
- confirm whether your current plan changed or stayed the same

## Keep billing guidance durable

Your exact pricing, limits, and billing options depend on your current plan. Check the billing screens in your workspace for the current source of truth.

<Info>
  If a qualification or enrichment action is unavailable, check both your credits and your workspace billing status before you troubleshoot anything else. For what happens after credits run out, see [Pay-as-you-go](/using-traxy/usage-based-credits).
</Info>
