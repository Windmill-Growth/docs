Use the billing area to understand how your workspace is billed and how credits affect lead actions.

## What you can manage

In **Settings** \> **Billing**, you can manage:

- your current subscription
- payment methods
- billing history
- plan changes
- pay-as-you-go settings when your plan supports them

Your workspace sidebar also shows credit usage so you can track consumption without leaving your day-to-day workflow.

Everyone in the workspace can follow usage from the product, but owners and admins manage billing changes.

## How credits are used

Credits affect lead qualification and contact enrichment across the workspace.

- Qualification usage is counted per unique person or profile during your current billing period, not per lead row.
- A person can still use qualification credits even if they do not end up as a qualified lead, because `traxy` still completed the evaluation.
- During onboarding, the first qualification run is free.

## How periods and resets work

Included credits reset with your workspace billing period.

When credits are low or unavailable, some actions pause until:

- your billing period resets
- you update billing settings
- you change plan or credit behavior

Use the billing summary in your workspace as the source of truth for the current period and remaining usage.

## Pay-as-you-go

If your workspace is on a paid plan, admins and owners can choose whether `traxy` keeps working after included credits are used.

- Pay-as-you-go is available on paid plans only.
- Lite workspaces stay inside included credits.
- If overage charges are already waiting to be billed, you may need to let that billing finish before you can turn pay-as-you-go off.

## Common billing tasks

Use the billing area when you need to:

- review current plan details
- update a card
- view invoices and payment history
- change subscription level
- open the billing portal for additional billing tasks

## Keep billing guidance durable

Your exact pricing, limits, and billing options depend on your current plan. Check the billing screens in your workspace for the current source of truth.

<Info>
  If a qualification or enrichment action is unavailable, check both your credits and your workspace billing status before you troubleshoot anything else.
</Info>
