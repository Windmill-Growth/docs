Use Profiles & Users and the settings area to manage who has access and how your team works inside `traxy`.

  ![Profilev4](/images/Profilev4.png)
</Frame>

## Create a workspace

When you create a new workspace, `traxy` makes you the **Owner**.

## Invite members

Owners and admins can invite members from:

- the sidebar action for inviting a team member
- **Settings** \> **Profiles & Users**

When you send an invite, choose the access level that fits the person joining:

- **Admin** for workspace setup and management
- **Member** for day-to-day work in the product

In the members view, you can track statuses such as:

- **Active**
- **Invite Pending**
- **Disabled**

## Manage roles

`traxy` uses workspace roles to control access.

- **Owner** has full workspace control.
- **Admin** can manage key workspace settings and members.
- **Member** works inside the workspace without full admin access.

If your role allows it, you can update roles or remove access from the same members screen.

Removing access disables that member immediately.

## Update profile and company settings

Use **Settings** to manage personal and workspace information such as:

- **Profile**
- **Company**
- **Security**
- **Billing**
- **Pay-as-you-go**
- **Blacklist**
- **Profiles & Users**

These settings help keep your workspace accurate as your team changes.

Use **Company** when you want to review the confirmed company record that powers your workspace.

Use **Blacklist** when you want to exclude specific company domains from future leads.
