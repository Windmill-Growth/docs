Use the content calendar when you want to inspect performance one post at a time instead of working from the full leads list.

## Use the month view

The content calendar shows your posts by month and lets you move backward or forward across available months.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot2026 03 16at11 30 00PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at11.30.00PM.png)
</Frame>

Use it to:

- review posting activity by day
- compare post performance over time
- inspect engagement before you qualify it

## Track performance

<Frame>
  ![Postlikes 1](/images/postlikes-1.png)
</Frame>

The performance area helps you compare monthly activity. Depending on the view, you can review:

- posts
- likes
- comments
- reposts

## Qualify engagement from a post

When a post has engagement, you can qualify that engagement directly from the calendar.

This is useful when:

- you want to review one post at a time
- you want to prioritize a post with strong buyer signals
- you want to refresh lead discovery after a high-performing post

Only posts with engagement can be qualified. Qualification actions also use credits.

## Review post analytics

Open post analytics to see the qualified leads tied to a specific post. From there, you can jump back into the leads view to continue working those people.

  ![Screenshot2026 03 16at11 31 45PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-16at11.31.45PM.png)

<Tip>
  Use the content calendar to find posts with strong engagement but weak lead quality. That usually signals an ICP or threshold adjustment opportunity.
</Tip>
