---
title: "Usage-based credits"
description: "Control what happens after your included monthly credits run out in traxy."
---

Use Usage-based credits when you want `traxy` to keep working after your included monthly credits are exhausted.

## Where to manage it

Open **Settings** > **Usage-based credits**.

Admins manage this setting for the workspace.

## What it does

When Usage-based credits is enabled, `traxy` can continue qualifying leads after your included monthly credits run out.

This helps you:

- keep automatic lead qualification running
- continue manual enrichment from the leads table or lead details view
- avoid stopping lead capture at the end of your included monthly credits

## When to use it

Usage-based credits is useful when:

- you want continuous lead capture
- your team is actively enriching leads and does not want to wait for the next reset
- you are close to your monthly limit but still want new qualified leads routed automatically

## What happens when it is off

If Usage-based credits is off and your workspace runs out of included monthly credits:

- qualification can pause
- some newly found leads can stay locked or hidden
- enrichment actions can become unavailable

At that point, the usual next steps are:

- wait for the billing period reset
- upgrade your plan
- or turn on Usage-based credits

## Keep the guidance durable

The app shows the current rules, costs, and confirmation steps for your workspace. Use that screen as the source of truth for the exact current behavior.

<Info>
  Usage-based credits is available on paid plans only.
</Info>
