Use Pay-as-you-go when you want `traxy` to keep qualifying and enriching leads after your included monthly credits are exhausted.

## Where to manage it

Open **Settings** \> **Pay-as-you-go**.

Admins manage this setting for the workspace.

<Frame>
  ![Credits](/images/Credits.png)
</Frame>

## What it does

When Pay-as-you-go is enabled, `traxy` can continue working after your included monthly credits run out.

This helps you:

- keep automatic lead qualification running
- continue manual enrichment from the leads table or lead details view
- avoid stopping lead capture at the end of your included monthly credits

## Track spend and budget

The Pay-as-you-go settings page shows your current-month spend and whether Pay-as-you-go is active.

Admins can also add an optional monthly budget cap.

Use a monthly budget when you want Pay-as-you-go available, but still want a ceiling on extra spend for the billing period.

## When to use it

Pay-as-you-go is useful when:

- you want continuous lead capture
- your team is actively enriching leads and does not want to wait for the next reset
- you are close to your monthly limit but still want new qualified leads routed automatically

## What happens when it is off or capped

If Pay-as-you-go is off and your workspace runs out of included monthly credits:

- qualification can pause
- some newly found leads can stay locked or hidden
- enrichment actions can become unavailable

If Pay-as-you-go is on but the monthly budget cap is reached:

- new Pay-as-you-go charges are blocked
- qualification or enrichment can pause again after included credits run out
- an admin can raise the budget or wait for the next reset

At that point, the usual next steps are:

- wait for the billing period reset
- upgrade your plan
- raise the monthly budget
- or turn on Pay-as-you-go

## Keep the guidance durable

The app shows the current pricing, action costs, spend summary, and confirmation steps for your workspace. Use that screen as the source of truth for the exact current behavior.

<Info>
  Pay-as-you-go is available on paid plans only.
</Info>
