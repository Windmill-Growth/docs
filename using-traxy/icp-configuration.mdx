Use ICP Configuration when you want to improve lead quality, widen lead volume, or adapt `traxy` to a new market.

## What you can edit

`traxy` splits ICP criteria into two parts:

- **People Criteria** for the individual you want to reach
- **Company Criteria** for the company you want to target

## People criteria

Use People Criteria to define the kind of person who should count as a fit.

The current public fields are:

- **Job Titles**
- **Departments**
- **Locations**

## Company criteria

Use Company Criteria to define the kind of company that should count as a fit.

The current public fields are:

- **Company Size**
- **Industries**
- **Revenue**
- **Funding**
- **Keywords & Niches**

## Update your ICP safely

Make small changes and then watch the results in **Dashboard**, **Leads**, and **Post Signals**.

- narrow your ICP when you are getting too many weak fits
- broaden your ICP when engagement exists but qualified leads stay low
- change one field at a time when you want cleaner feedback on what improved

## When to revisit it

Revisit ICP Configuration when:

- your team changes who it wants to target
- your content is attracting attention from the wrong audience
- you move into a new segment or geography
- lead quality changes after a campaign or content shift

<Tip>
  Your ICP and your workspace threshold work together. If lead quality is close but volume is low, review both your ICP and your current dashboard threshold before making a bigger change.
</Tip>
