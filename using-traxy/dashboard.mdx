The dashboard gives you a high-level view of how `traxy` is turning LinkedIn engagement into qualified leads.

## What you can monitor

The main metric cards show:

- **Total Leads**
- **Perfect Leads**
- **Total Followers**
- **Qualified Engagement Rate**

If your role allows it, you can also adjust the ICP match threshold from the dashboard. Lower thresholds increase volume. Higher thresholds increase strictness.

## Adjust the ICP threshold

The dashboard uses your current workspace ICP threshold when it counts leads.

- Raise the threshold when you want stricter matching.
- Lower the threshold when you want to review more borderline matches.
- Watch both **Total Leads** and **Perfect Leads** after you change it.

## Review perfect leads

The perfect leads section highlights the highest-fit people in your workspace. Use it to:

- spot the strongest matches first
- open a lead directly in **Leads**
- check whether your fit threshold is too strict

If `traxy` finds leads but none match your perfect lead criteria, review your ICP or lower the threshold.

## Review top posts

The top posts area helps you understand which LinkedIn posts are producing the best lead outcomes.

The tracked-post insight is a rolling 7-day view. It highlights qualified leads from tracked posts in the last 7 days and, when enough history exists, compares recent engagement with the previous 7 days.

Use it to:

- compare posts by qualified lead count
- open post-level analytics
- jump from a post into the related leads

The dashboard can keep updating while your first qualification pass is still running, so it is a good place to monitor onboarding progress without leaving the app.

If no posts qualify yet, the dashboard may still show engagement trends that help you decide what to publish next.

## Use dashboard signals to troubleshoot

Common dashboard states include:

- no posts analyzed yet
- low engagement
- engagement without qualified leads

If the dashboard looks empty or lead counts stay low, continue with [no qualified leads yet](/troubleshooting/no-qualified-leads-yet).
