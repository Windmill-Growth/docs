Use this page when someone on your team invites you into an existing `traxy` workspace.

## What to expect

When an admin invites you, `traxy` sends an invite link tied to that workspace.

Before you accept, the invite stays pending. If it expires, is revoked, or was sent to the wrong address, an admin can send a new one.

## Join a workspace

1. Open the invite link from your email or shared invite message.
2. Sign in, or create your account, with the same email address that received the invite.
3. If `traxy` asks you to confirm your email before the invite can be accepted, finish that step first.
4. Review the workspace name and your role before you continue.
5. Accept the invite to add the workspace to your account.
6. Use the workspace switcher if you already belong to more than one workspace.

Invited members usually have a shorter onboarding flow than someone creating a new workspace. You may only need to verify your LinkedIn profile and wait for workspace access to finish.

## What happens after you join

Each workspace keeps its own:

- ICP configuration
- leads
- integrations
- billing and usage settings

Your role controls what you can change inside that workspace.

- **Owner** has full workspace control.
- **Admin** can manage key workspace settings and members.
- **Member** works inside the workspace without full admin access.

## If you cannot join

Check these first:

- You are signing in with the invited email address.
- The invite link is still valid.
- The workspace admin has not revoked and reissued the invite.
- You finished any required email verification.

If the invite still does not work, ask an admin to resend it.
