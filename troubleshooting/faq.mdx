---
title: "FAQ"
description: "Answers to common public user questions about traxy."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is a qualified lead?">
    A qualified lead is a person whose LinkedIn engagement matches your workspace ICP strongly enough to count as a fit in `traxy`.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is a qualified engagement?">
    Qualified engagement is engagement from people who meet your workspace fit criteria closely enough to matter for lead qualification.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where do I update my ICP?">
    You can update your ICP during onboarding or later in **Settings** > **ICP Configuration**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is Slack connected but not sending alerts?">
    Slack notifications stay disabled until you select a channel and enable the trigger.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What does the Webhook integration do?">
    Webhook sends qualified lead events from `traxy` to your endpoint so another system can receive and act on them.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can I not qualify a post?">
    The post may have no engagement, qualification data may still be refreshing, or your workspace may not have enough credits for the action.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I work across multiple workspaces?">
    Yes. If you belong to more than one workspace, use the workspace switcher in the sidebar to move between them.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where do I manage billing and credits?">
    Open **Settings** > **Billing** for subscription tasks and use the sidebar usage card for day-to-day credit visibility.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
