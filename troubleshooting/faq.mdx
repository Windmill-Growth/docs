---
title: "FAQ"
description: "Answers to common public user questions about traxy."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is a qualified lead?">
    A qualified lead is a person whose LinkedIn engagement matches your workspace ICP strongly enough to count as a fit in `traxy`.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is a qualified engagement?">
    Qualified engagement is engagement from people who meet your workspace fit criteria closely enough to matter for lead qualification.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why did credits change even when I did not get new qualified leads?">
    Qualification credits can be used when `traxy` evaluates a unique person during the current billing period, even if that person does not end up as a qualified lead.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where do I update my ICP?">
    You can update your ICP during onboarding or later in **ICP Configuration** from the main app navigation.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is Slack connected but not sending alerts?">
    Slack notifications stay disabled until you select a channel and enable the trigger. If you chose a private channel, invite the `traxy` Slack app to that channel before you test it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What does the Webhook integration do?">
    Webhook sends qualified lead events from `traxy` to your endpoint so another system can receive and act on them.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is auto-send not delivering older leads?">
    Auto-send only applies to leads that qualify after it is enabled. Use manual delivery from the leads table if you want to send existing leads.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use more than one Webhook?">
    No. Each workspace supports one active Webhook integration at a time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can I not qualify a post?">
    The post may have no engagement, qualification data may still be refreshing, or your workspace may not have enough credits for the action.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why are some leads locked or missing after I hit my credit limit?">
    If qualification is paused because your workspace ran out of available credits, some newly found leads can stay locked until credits reset, your plan changes, or an admin enables Pay-as-you-go.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I work across multiple workspaces?">
    Yes. If you belong to more than one workspace, use the workspace switcher in the sidebar to move between them.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where do I manage billing and usage settings?">
    Open **Settings** > **Billing** for subscription tasks, credit usage history, payment methods, and invoices. Open **Settings** > **Pay-as-you-go** for overage behavior and monthly budget controls.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
