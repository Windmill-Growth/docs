---
title: "No qualified leads yet"
description: "Troubleshoot low lead volume or missing qualified leads in traxy."
---

If you are seeing little or no qualified lead activity, work through these checks in order.

## 1. Check whether processing is still running

After onboarding, `traxy` needs time to process recent activity and match it against your ICP.

Start with:

- **Dashboard** for overall progress
- **Leads** for newly added people
- **Post Signals** for post-level review

If you recently changed your ICP or qualified a post, give the workspace time to refresh.

## 2. Review your ICP

An ICP that is too narrow is the most common reason for low lead volume.

Review:

- people criteria such as job titles, departments, and locations
- company criteria such as size, industry, revenue, funding, and keywords

If engagement exists but qualified leads do not, broaden one field at a time and monitor the results.

## 3. Check your fit threshold

If your workspace uses a high ICP match threshold, borderline matches may never become qualified leads.

If your role allows it, reduce the threshold slightly and watch whether lead volume improves without hurting quality.

## 4. Check source activity

Low posting volume or low engagement can also limit lead discovery.

Look for these dashboard patterns:

- no posts analyzed
- low engagement
- engagement without qualified leads

Use Post Signals to find posts with real activity and run **Find Leads** directly when appropriate.

## 5. Check credits

The onboarding first run is free, but ongoing qualification and enrichment actions depend on credits. If credits are exhausted or billing is paused, lead discovery actions may be limited.

Open [Billing & Plans](/using-traxy/billing-and-credits) and [Usage-based credits](/using-traxy/usage-based-credits) and confirm your workspace can still run the actions you need.

## 6. Confirm your downstream setup

If leads exist in `traxy` but are not reaching Slack or your Webhook, the issue may be in delivery rather than qualification.

Check:

- Slack channel selection
- Slack app access to private channels
- trigger status
- Webhook endpoint configuration
- test delivery results

<Tip>
  If you are getting engagement but not enough qualified leads, start by widening your ICP before you change your publishing strategy.
</Tip>
