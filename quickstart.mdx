Use this page to move from onboarding to your first review of qualified leads.

## Before you begin

- You need access to a `traxy` workspace.
- You should have your personal LinkedIn profile URL ready.
- You should know the people and companies you want to target.

## Complete setup

1. Add your personal LinkedIn profile during onboarding.
   traxy verifies your profile before you can continue.
2. Review the ICP traxy generates for your workspace.
   Adjust it before you activate it if the target audience is too broad or too narrow.
3. Wait for workspace setup to finish.
   The setup page stays open while traxy prepares your workspace and starts background processing.
4. Open **Dashboard**.
   Use it to monitor lead volume, fit quality, and top-performing posts.
5. Open **Leads**.
   Use it to review the people traxy has identified and take action.
6. Connect **Slack** or **Webhook** if you want lead alerts or downstream delivery.

## What happens next

- Your dashboard updates as new activity is processed.
- Your leads list grows as traxy finds more people who match your ICP.
- Your content calendar lets you qualify engagement from specific posts by day.
- Your billing area shows credit usage, subscription details, and payment methods.

<Info>
  If you do not see qualified leads yet, start with the [no qualified leads yet](/troubleshooting/no-qualified-leads-yet) troubleshooting guide.
</Info>
