Use this page to move from onboarding to your first review of qualified leads.

## Before you begin

- You need access to a `traxy` workspace.
- You should have your personal LinkedIn profile URL ready.
- You should know the people and companies you want to target.

If you were invited into an existing workspace, start with [join a workspace](/getting-started/join-a-workspace).

## Complete setup

1. Create or join your workspace.
   If you create a workspace, you become the **Owner**. If you were invited, you may have a shorter onboarding path.
2. Add your personal LinkedIn profile during onboarding.
   `traxy` verifies your profile before setup can continue.
3. Confirm your company.
   If `traxy` cannot confirm the right company automatically, review the suggested company or verify the domain before you continue.
4. Review your recent LinkedIn performance.
   Onboarding shows a recent content summary so you can confirm `traxy` is looking at the right profile and activity.
5. Choose a plan if your onboarding flow asks for it.
   Invited members usually skip this step.
6. Review the ICP draft `traxy` generates for your workspace.
   Tighten it if the target audience is too broad. Broaden it if it looks too narrow.
7. Let onboarding finish the first qualification pass.
   `traxy` analyzes engagement from your last 5 LinkedIn posts for free and starts matching that activity against your ICP.
8. Wait for the workspace finalizing step if it appears.
   `traxy` may briefly show a setup screen while it finishes workspace creation in the background.
9. Open **Dashboard**.
   Use it to monitor lead volume, fit quality, and top-performing posts.
10. Open **Leads**.
   Use it to review the people `traxy` identified and decide what to do next.
11. Open **Post Signals**.
   Use it to review activity by post and run **Find Leads** on specific posts with engagement.
12. Connect **Slack** or **Webhook** if you want alerts or downstream delivery.

## What happens next

- Your dashboard updates as new activity is processed.
- Your leads list grows as `traxy` finds more people who match your ICP.
- Post Signals lets you find leads from specific posts by day.
- Billing and settings show your current plan, usage, and workspace controls.

<Info>
  If you do not see qualified leads yet, start with the [no qualified leads yet](/troubleshooting/no-qualified-leads-yet) troubleshooting guide.
</Info>
