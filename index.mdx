## Start here

1. Complete the [quickstart](/quickstart).
2. Review or update your [ICP](/getting-started/define-your-icp).
3. Open [Dashboard](/using-traxy/dashboard) and [Leads](/using-traxy/leads) once processing starts.
4. Configure [Integrations](/using-traxy/integrations) when you are ready to route leads downstream.

<Tip>
  Keep your first ICP clear and realistic. If it is too narrow, you may see engagement without many qualified leads.
</Tip>
