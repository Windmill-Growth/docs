## Start here

1. Join or create a workspace, then complete the [quickstart](/quickstart).
2. Review or update your [ICP](/getting-started/define-your-icp).
3. Let `traxy` finish the first qualification pass from your latest LinkedIn posts.
4. Open [Dashboard](/using-traxy/dashboard), [Leads](/using-traxy/leads), and [Post Signals](/using-traxy/content-calendar) while first-run processing completes.
5. Configure [Integrations](/using-traxy/integrations) when you are ready to route leads downstream.

<Tip>
  Your onboarding first run is meant to give you early signal quickly. If volume stays low after it completes, widen your ICP before you assume something is broken.
</Tip>
